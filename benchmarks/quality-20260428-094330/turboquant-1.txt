To solve the expression $23 \times 47 + 18$, we must follow the order of operations (PEMDAS/BODMAS), which dictates that multiplication is performed before addition.

**Step 1: Perform the multiplication ($23 \times 47$)**

We can calculate this using standard long multiplication or by breaking it down:

$$
\begin{array}{r}
  47 \\
\times 23 \\
\hline
  141 & (3 \times 47) \\
+ 940 & (20 \times 47) \\
\hline
 1081
\end{array}
$$

So, $23 \times 47 = 1081$.

**Step 2: Perform the addition ($1081 + 18$)**

Now, add 18 to the result from Step 1:

$$
\begin{array}{r}
  1081 \\
+ \quad 18 \\
\hline
  1099
\end{array}
$$

**Final Conclusion:**

$$ 23 \times 47 + 18 = 1099 $$
